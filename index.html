Free Online Piano & Harmonium

WebKeys is a free virtual piano keyboard you can play directly in your browser — no download, no signup, no plugins required. Click any key, tap on mobile, or use your computer keyboard to play real notes with full audio synthesis powered by the Web Audio API.

Instruments

Choose from four instruments: Piano (warm triangle-wave harmonics), Organ (sustained sine drawbars), Synth (filtered sawtooth), and Harmonium (detuned reed ranks with bellows attack — perfect for Indian classical music practice).

Features

Adjust reverb for concert-hall depth, change the key across all 12 semitones, shift the octave across 7 ranges, control sustain and volume, and filter the keyboard to highlight only Major, Minor, or Pentatonic scale notes — ideal for learning and improvising.
